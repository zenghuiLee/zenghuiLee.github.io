Humans of China│人在中国（九十三）

发表于 Dec 10, 2018

Humans of China
人在中国 Exploring China one person at a time 一次探索一个中国人

From： Cameron Hack CJHphotography

I used to be a solider but after giving birth too my second child I lost my job. I had a second child because I wanted a boy but the second child was a girl. After losing my job I was angry and so we had a third child, which, was a boy and I was very happy. Back then if you broke the strict 1 child policy law then you could lose your job. Being a solider was great, I met many good people and now I have friends all over China. Now I drive a taxi, I live with my dog and often see my children who, I am all very proud of as they are very successful. Although I married, I can’t live with my wife because the dog can’t live in flat we own so, instead me and the dog live in the bungalow together. I kind of regret buying her but I still take good care of her and I see my wife everyday. When lunch is ready she’ll give me a call and I’ll go home and we’ll eat together.

Tianjin

我以前是个军人，但是我有了第二个孩子后，就被辞退了。我选择要第二个孩子是因为我们想生个男孩，但生的还是女孩。我失业后，很生气，所以我们又有了第三个孩子，第三个孩子是个男孩子，我很开心。我以前当兵的时候，如果你违反了独生子的政策，你就会失业。当兵很好，我遇到过很多好人，我现在在全中国都有朋友。我现在开出租车，我还养了一条狗，我经常去看我的孩子们，他们很成功，我很为他们骄傲。虽然我结婚了，但是我不能和我妻子住一起，因为狗不能住在我们楼房里，所以我就带着狗住在平房里面。我有点后悔买这条狗，但是我还在好好照顾她。我每天都回家看我的妻子。午饭做好的时候，她就给我打电话，我就回家和她一起吃饭。

天津
